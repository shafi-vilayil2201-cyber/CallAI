📖 Local Testing Instructions
  • 1
    Ensure your local database is initialized and seeded. Open a terminal in the root and run: npx prisma migrate dev && npx prisma db seed
  • 2
    Open a terminal and start the NestJS backend in development mode: pnpm run start:dev
  • 3
    If testing the actual AI responses, ensure your OPENAI_API_KEY is exported in your environment.
  • 4
    Click Start Sandbox Call above. The browser will ask for microphone access, establish a real-time WebSocket connection to the gateway, and connect your mic!
